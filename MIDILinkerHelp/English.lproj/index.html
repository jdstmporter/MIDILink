About MIDILinker

MIDILinker is a simple tool that lets you create links between MIDI devices in or attached to your computer. It's just like a virtual MIDI patch-board. You can link any output port to any input port with three clicks, and can even have multiple links coming out of / going into one port.

In addition, MIDILinker contains a simple MIDI monitor, so you can watch the MIDI messages flowing across any of the links you have created.

Using MIDILinker

  • Listing MIDI endpoints
  • Linking and unlinking MIDI endpoints
  • Monitoring linked endpoints
