Listing Endpoints

The left-hand pane of MIDILinker lists MIDI endpoints currently active on or attached to your Mac. At the top are source endpoints and at the bottom are destination endpoints. For each endpoint, the panel lists:

  • UID : the endpoint unique identifier
  • Name : the endpoint name
  • Model : the endpoint (ort he device it's a part of) model
  • Manufacturer : the endpoint (or the device it's a part of) manufacturer

If you have added / removed endpoimts, or just want to refresh the list, click on the Scan button below the lists.
