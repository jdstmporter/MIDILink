Monitoring a Link

MIDILink gives the capability to monitor, in real time, MIDI messages passing across links that you have created. To monitor a link, double click on it in the links panel, and the monitor window will emerge, listing commands in himan-friendly format as they occur.

You can scroll up and down the list of messages received since monitoring began. If it becomes too long, you can clear the log by clicking the 'reset' button.

To stop monitoring, double click the link again, and the monitor window will vanish.
